FAQs

What does a financial representative do?

As a Lyfe financial representative, you help individuals and families plan their financial future through insurance, savings, and investment solutions. You’ll build lasting client relationships and provide personalised advice.

Do I need prior experience in insurance or finance?

No prior experience is required. We provide comprehensive training and mentorship to help you build the skills and knowledge needed to succeed.

What training and certifications are provided?

We support you through all required certifications including RES (Rules & Ethics of Securities), M5 (Life Insurance), HI (Health Insurance), and M9/M9A (General Insurance). Training materials and exam prep are included.

How does compensation work?

You'll earn through a combination of base support during your initial period and commissions on your advisory work. Top performers enjoy uncapped earning potential.

What's the career progression path?

New advisors start as Financial Representatives. With experience and results, you can progress to Senior Representative, Manager, Director, and beyond — each with expanded responsibilities and earnings.

Where is the office located?

Lyfe is based in Singapore. We operate with a flexible model — you'll split time between client meetings, the office, and remote work.

What support do new agents receive?

Every new representative is paired with an experienced mentor. You'll have access to training programmes, exam preparation resources, team meetings, and ongoing professional development.

How do I apply?

Click 'Apply Now' to start your application. You'll complete a short profile, take a personality assessment, and then meet with our team to discuss your goals.

What's the interview process like?

After submitting your application, you'll complete a DISC personality assessment. Then you'll have a conversation with a manager to explore whether Lyfe is the right fit for you.

Is this a full-time or flexible role?

We offer flexibility. While building your practice requires commitment, you have control over your schedule and how you manage your time.
